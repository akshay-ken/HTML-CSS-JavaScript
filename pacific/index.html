Enjoy Nature in Luxury

Pacific Trails Resort offers a special lodging experience on the California Nort Coast with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean. Your stay at Pacific Trails Resort includes a sumptuously appointed private yurt and a cooked-to-order breakfast each morning.

  • Relax in out lodge while enjoying complimentary appetizers and beverages.
  • Savor nightly fine dining with an ocean view.
  • Unwind in the heated outdoor pool and whirlpool.
  • Explore the coast on your own or join our guided tours.
